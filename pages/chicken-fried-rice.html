Healthy recipe for the BEST Chicken Fried Rice

Recipe:

  • 3 cups of cooked white rice
  • 1lb of boneless chicken breast, diced into 3/4 inch pieces
  • 1 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp canola oil
  • 1 1/2 cups of frozen vegetable mix
  • 3 Green onions chopped
  • 2 diced cloves of garlic
  • 3 Large eggs
  • 3 Tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Sriracha for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large non-stick pan/ wok, heat up 1 1/2 tsp of both canola and sesame oil over medium-high heat. Once hot add chicken, season with light salt and pepper and saute until cooked through. Move to plate and set aside.
  2. Return heat to medium and add remaining canola and sesame oil. Add veggies and let saute for 1 minute. Add garlic and saute for another minute. Push veggies to the side and add eggs to the middle to scramble.
  3. Return chicken to heat. Add soy sauce, salt and pepper to taste. Serve with sriracha if desired.

Macros! (per serving)

  • Calories: 520
  • Protein: 36g
  • Carbs: 54g
  • Fat: 17g
