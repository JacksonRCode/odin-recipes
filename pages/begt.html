Bacon, eggs, guacamole, and toast recipe!!

Ingredients:

  • Guacamole
  • Two strips of bacon
  • Five eggs
  • Handful of spinach
  • Tablespoon of milk
  • Two slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Recipe:

  1. Chop bacon slices in half vertically and horizontally, and then into small bits
  2. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk together with milk
  3. Chop spinach up however much you'd like
  4. Heat pan up to high and add bacon; continuously stirring
  5. When it crisps up add spinach and some pepper. Let cook for 30-60 seconds
  6. Turn down heat and add eggs, continuously stirring
  7. Take off heat and add salt and more pepper
  8. Put toast in toaster, and once it's done spread guacamole on it
  9. The the scramble on top and it's ready to serve
  10. Tastes good with hot sauce or ketchup!
