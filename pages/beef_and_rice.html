Beef and Rice Recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Two pounds of BEEF
  • Two cans of black beans
  • Two cans of CORN
  • Diced medium sized onion
  • Two packets of taco seasoning
  • Two cups of rice
  • 2 Tablespoons of olive oil

Recipe:

  1. Cook rice in a rice cooker
  2. Add olive oil to a medium high pan and wait for it to heat up
  3. Add onion to pan and cook until it begins to shine
  4. Add beef and cook until half brown half pink
  5. Strain and wash beans and corn, and then add to pan
  6. Add taco seasoning after two minutes
  7. Add cooked rice to the pan
  8. Mix and serve!
  9. Good with guacamole, hot sauce, and parsley
