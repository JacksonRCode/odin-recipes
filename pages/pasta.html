Sausage Pasta Recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Two pounds of sausage
  • Two small packets of spaghetti
  • Red pasta sauce
  • Two tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt

Recipe:

  1. Cut up sausages into small chunks, continuously stirring
  2. Add oil to pan until warmed up
  3. Add sausage chunks to pan
  4. Boil water and salt it
  5. Add pasta to water for 10-15 mins until el dante
  6. Strain pasta and add back to pot
  7. Add sausage and pasta to sauce once finished cooking
  8. Serve!
